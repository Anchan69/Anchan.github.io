About Me

I'm a robotics engineer based in Kathmandu, Nepal with 1.5 years of hands-on experience building autonomous systems, sensors, and embedded hardware from the ground up.

Currently preparing for FGC 2026 in Incheon, South Korea — the "Olympics of Robotics" — by mastering PID control, OpenCV, and Onshape CAD to earn a spot on Team Nepal.

I believe in practical over theoretical. Every circuit I build and every bot I program is a step toward putting Nepal on the global robotics map.
